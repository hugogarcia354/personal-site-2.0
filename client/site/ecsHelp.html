How To Use The Site

Choose Your Interests!

  1. Navigate to Account
  2. Select "Change Interest Tags"
  3. Select all the Interests that you are interested in.
  4. Click on Save

Read Articles and Earn Points!

  1. Have Interest Tags selected!
  2. Click on the toggle button
  3. Click on an Interest
  4. Click on an article that you want to read

  5. Earn Points!

      6. *Points are earned after reading for 3 minutes and 30 seconds

Enter Sweepstakes

  1. Navigate to Sweepstakes
  2. Click on "Is Sweepstake Open" to check if a sweepstake is on, and to check the cost and prize
  3. Click on "What are your Points?" to see if you can afford the ticket
  4. Buy a ticket!
  5. When the raffle ends, you will be notified if you win!

Edit Your Information

  1. Navigate to Account
  2. Select "General Information" to edit your personal Information
  3. Select "Change Password" to change your Password
  4. Select "Change Interest Tags" to modify your interests
